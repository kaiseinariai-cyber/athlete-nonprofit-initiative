About Us

Why this initiative matters

Student athletes work hard to improve their performance, but many do not receive enough education about injury prevention, recovery, and long-term health. Small habits such as proper warm-ups, safe training, and recovery basics can make a big difference.

Our initiative exists to help athletes build healthy habits, reduce preventable injuries, and gain access to helpful resources and support.

Mission

Helping athletes stay healthy, safe, and informed

Our mission is to raise awareness about athlete health and injury prevention while supporting student athletes through education, outreach, and community involvement.

Leadership Roles

Student leadership structure

Our initiative is powered by student leaders working together across education, outreach, operations, events, and community partnerships.

Education Lead

Develops workshop content and helps ensure information is accurate, clear, and helpful.

Marketing Lead

Manages social media, promotes events, and supports public outreach.

Partnerships Lead

Builds relationships with coaches, trainers, physical therapists, and community partners.

Operations Lead

Coordinates meetings, internal communication, and team organization.

Outreach Lead

Connects with teams, schools, coaches, and organizations to schedule workshops and expand impact.

Events Lead

Plans logistics and helps run workshops and events smoothly.

Fundraising Lead

Organizes fundraising efforts and helps track goals and ideas.
